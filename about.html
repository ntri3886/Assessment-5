About Four Paws

Our vision is a world in which humans treat animals with respect, empathy and understanding.

  • Early Days

    Founded in 1988, Austria in resposnse to the cruelty of fur farms. Early on we were successful in closing down fur farms and stoping animal transportation. We also generated awareness of the suffering of bears.

  • Accomplishments

    Currently we have 13 offices & Runn 5 bear sanctuaries globally. The Four Paws LIONSROCK sanctuary in South Africa provides a home to over 100 rescued big cats. The bear sanctuary in asia houses 83 bears.

  • Our Vision

    For many years, Four Paws has been on the forefront in improving the way wild animals are kept. An importnat part of this mission is the legal ban of private ownership of bears.

  • Our Projects

    Four Paws has launched a project to save bile bears in Vietnam. It is estimated 1,800 to 1,900 bears are still living in appalling cruel conditions. We are currently working with the Vietnamese government to provide a home for 100 bears.
